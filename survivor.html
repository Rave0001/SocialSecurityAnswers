Social Security Survivor Benefits Guide

Survivor benefits provide financial support to family members after a worker dies. Here’s a simple overview:

  • Widow(er)s: Up to 100% of the deceased’s benefit at FRA, or 71.5% at age 60.
  • Children: 75% of the benefit if under 18 (or 19 in school).
  • Apply: Call 1-800-772-1213 or visit an office. Survivor Info.
