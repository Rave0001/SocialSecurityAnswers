Housing Assistance

Get help with affordable housing through these programs:

  • Section 8 (Housing Choice Voucher): Vouchers subsidize rent for low-income families, elderly, and disabled individuals. You choose your housing (apartments, houses) if the landlord accepts vouchers.
  • Public Housing: Affordable apartments/units owned by local housing agencies for eligible low-income households.
  • Eligibility: Income typically ≤ 50% of area median income (e.g., $36,750 for a family of 4 in 2025, varies by location). Priority for very low-income (≤ 30%).
  • Apply: Contact your local Public Housing Agency (PHA). Waitlists may apply due to high demand.
