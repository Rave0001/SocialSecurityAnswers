Housing Assistance - Section 8 and More

Housing assistance helps low-income families afford a place to live:

  • Section 8: Vouchers to help pay rent. Income ≤ 50% area median.
  • Apply: Contact your local Public Housing Agency (PHA). Find your PHA.
