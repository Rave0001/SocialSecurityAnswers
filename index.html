Get a Replacement or New Social Security Card

Need to replace your Social Security card or get a new one? It’s free! Here’s how:

  • Online: Fastest way if you’re 18+, have a U.S. address, and a My SSA account. Replace Card Online
  • Mail/In-Person: Use Form SS-5 if online isn’t an option. Download SS-5

Documents You’ll Need for Social Security Card Replacement

  • For Children: Birth certificate, proof of U.S. citizenship (if not on birth record), parent’s ID. Child Docs
  • For Adults: Birth certificate or passport, driver’s license or state ID. Adult Docs
  • For Name Change: Marriage/divorce decree, court order, or other legal name change doc, plus ID. Name Change Docs

Note: You might need an appointment for in-person visits. Call 1-800-772-1213 or book online at SSA Contact.
