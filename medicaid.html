Medicaid Info

Basics of Medicaid:

  • Who Qualifies: Low-income, disabled, elderly, kids.
  • Coverage: Free/low-cost health care.
  • Apply: Via state Medicaid office.

Medicaid FAQs

Who can get SNAP with Medicaid?

If you’re on Medicaid, you may qualify for SNAP if your income is below 130% of the poverty line (e.g., $2,694/month for a family of 3 in 2025). More info.

What does the QMB program cover?

QMB pays Medicare Part A/B premiums, deductibles, and copays for low-income beneficiaries (income ≤ $1,275/month individual, 2025). Details.

Can I have Medicare and Medicaid?

Yes, dual eligibility means Medicare pays first, Medicaid covers extras (e.g., premiums, long-term care). Learn more.
