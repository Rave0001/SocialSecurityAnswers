Medicaid Information - Health Coverage Basics

Medicaid provides free or low-cost health coverage to eligible low-income individuals. Here’s what you need to know:

  • Eligibility: Low-income, disabled, elderly, children, or pregnant women—varies by state. Eligibility.
  • Coverage: Doctor visits, hospital stays, prescriptions—details vary by state.
  • Apply: Via your state’s Medicaid office or HealthCare.gov. Apply Info.
