Social Security Frequently Asked Questions

How do I replace my Social Security card?

Apply online at My SSA or use Form SS-5 by mail/in-person. See Home for details.

When can I start collecting retirement benefits?

At 62 (reduced), FRA (66-67), or 70 (increased). Check our Retirement Guide.

What’s the difference between SSDI and SSI?

SSDI is for workers with credits; SSI is for low-income individuals. See Disability Guide.
