Frequently Asked Questions

When can I start collecting Social Security?

At 62, reduced; full at 66-67. Learn more.

Can I work and collect benefits?

Yes, under FRA, $22,320 limit in 2025. Details.

How do I apply for Social Security?

Online, phone, or office. Apply.

What’s the maximum benefit?

$3,822/month in 2025 at FRA. Updates.

What happens if I delay benefits?

8% increase per year to 70. More.

Can I get benefits if divorced?

Yes, if married 10+ years. Details.

What’s SSDI vs. SSI?

SSDI for workers; SSI for low-income. More.

How are benefits taxed?

50-85% if income > $25,000 (single). Tax info.

When are payments made?

Based on birth date: 2nd, 3rd, or 4th Wednesday. Schedule.

How do I appeal a denial?

Within 60 days, online or mail. Process.

What’s the 2025 COLA?

2.5%, adding ~$48/month. Details.
